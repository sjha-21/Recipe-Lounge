Besan chilla (Gram Flour Pancakes)

It is also known as vegetarian omelette because of the colour and texture. It is one of the famous North Indian breakfasts and has similar characteristics to South Indian Dosa recipes. It is basically a quick and easy breakfast recipe which can be prepared in jiffy within minutes during those early busy mornings. Besan chilla can also be topped with chopped capsicum / bell peppers or even with carrots and beans.
Besan chilla makes a filling meal, is gluten-free and vegan, great way to sneak in the veggies, makes a great breakfast or snack option. Chilla is a great way to add lots of veggies to your diet. I have used onion, carrots and peppers here.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

5 servings

Labels : Light Breakfast

instructions

step 1

In a large mixing bowl take 1 cup besan, add in ¼ tsp turmeric, ¼ tsp ajwain and salt to taste. Aadd ½ cup water or little more. Mix with the help of whisk to form a smooth batter. Now rest the batter for 30 minutes or more so that besan absorbs water. turns light.

step 2

Add in ½ onion, ½ tomato, 2 tbsp coriander leaves, 1 inch ginger and 1 green chilli. Mix well with the help of ladle and get to a flowing consistency batter.

step 3

Pour a ladleful of batter onto a hot tawa and spread gently. Pour a tsp of oil over chilla. Allow to cook on medium flame. Now flip the chilla and cook both sides. Finally, fold the besan chilla and serve with mint chutney.

ingredients

1 cup besan (gram flour), ¼ tsp turmeric, ¼ tsp caraway seeds (ajwain), salt to taste, ½ cup water, ½ onion (finely chopped), 2 tbsp coriander leaves (finely chopped), ½ to mato (finely chopped), 1 inch ginger (finely chopped), 1 green chilli (finely chopped), 5 tsp oil (for roasting)

tools

Non-stick Pan

Large Mixing Bowl

Ladle
