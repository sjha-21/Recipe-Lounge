Chaas

The classic Chaas (Chaach/Mattha) also known as the Indian version of buttermilk is a refreshingly cooling summer drink made with creamy yogurt and seasonings.It is a traditional Indian chaas is made by blending cold, rich yogurt with a bit of water and nearly any spices you like.
This summer drink in known by various names all over India â€“ mattha (Hindi), taak (Marathi), chaach (Hindi), majjige (Kannada), moru (Tamil & Malayalam), majjiga (Telugu).

prep time

5 mins.

serving

2 servings

Labels : Drinks Breakfast Heavy

instructions

step 1

Add all ingredients to a blender.

step 2

Blend till combined.

step 3

Pour into serving glasses, garnish with fresh cilantro or mint and serve.

ingredients

1 cup plain yogurt
2 cups cold water

1 green chili
1/2 inch ginger
1 tablespoon chopped cilantro

1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
1/2 teaspoon black salt
1/4 teaspoon salt or to taste
1/4 teaspoon chat masala

tools

Blender

Glasses
