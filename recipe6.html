Punjabi Lassi

Lassi is a cooling, refreshing, probiotic drink perfect for the warm summer months. Popular in Punjab and North India, there are many variations of a lassi recipe, including mango lassi, dry fruits lassi, and malai lassi. Before you begin ensure that the whole milk curd (yogurt) is fresh and not gone sour. It is generally served after the spicy meal, to cool down the spice heat, but can also be served as a refreshing summer drink beverage.

prep time

5 mins.

serving

2 servings

Labels : Drinks Breakfast Heavy

instructions

step 1

In a blender take 2 cups curd (make sure the curd is thick and chilled).
Add 4-5 tbsp sugar, 1 tsp cardamom powder and 1 cup milk for thin consistensy and 1/2 cup for thick consistensy.

step 2

Blend to make sure everything is well combined.

step 3

Finally, pour punjabi lassi in a tall glass and top with malai or cardamom powder. Serve chilled

ingredients

2 cups curd (thick and not sour)

5 tbsp sugar

1 cup milk

1 tsp cardamom powder

tools

Blender

Glasses
